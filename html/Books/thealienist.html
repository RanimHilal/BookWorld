The Alienist

by Caleb Carr | Rate
Published:1994.
genre: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Fiction, Thriller, Crime, Historical, Mystery Thriller, Horror, Historical Mystery, Suspense.
The year is 1896, the place, New York City. On a cold March night New York Times reporter John Schuyler Moore is summoned to the East River by his friend and former Harvard classmate Dr. Laszlo Kreizler, a psychologist, or "alienist." On the unfinished Williamsburg Bridge,The plot of the novel revolves around a particularly gruesome crime when they view the horribly mutilated body of an adolescent boy.
