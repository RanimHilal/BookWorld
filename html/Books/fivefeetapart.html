Five Feet Apart

by Rachael Lippincott, Mikki Daughtry and Tobias Iaconis. | Rate
Published:2018.
genre: Romance, Young Adult, Contemporary, Fiction, Realistic Fiction, Young Adult Contemporary, Young Adult Romance, Teen.
In this moving story two teens fall in love with just one minor complication—they can’t get within five feet of each other without risking their lives. Can you love someone you can never touch? Stella Grant likes to be in control—even though her totally out of control lungs have sent her in and out of the hospital most of her life. At this point, what Stella needs to control most is keeping herself away from anyone or anything that might pass along an infection and jeopardize the possibility of a lung transplant. Six feet apart. No exceptions.
