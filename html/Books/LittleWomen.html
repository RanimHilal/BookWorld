Little Women

by Louisa May Alcott | Rate
Published:1868.
genre: Coming-of-age, Romance, Children's Literature.
This novel is about the lives of four sisters Meg, Jo, Beth and Amy, their love, growing up and finding their place in the world, during the American Civil War. Four sisters and their mother at home. Their father is fighting in the war. They are a close family who inevitably have their squabbles and tragedies. But the bond holds even when, later men friends start to become part of the household.
